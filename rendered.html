How we build

Boring on purpose, where it counts.

01

Specify before we build

Every module starts as a written specification your committee can read — 245 audit requirements and 80 share-and-dividend business rules exist on paper before they exist in code.

02

One core, not a patchwork

Membership, money and approvals share one engine and one database. Nothing is reconciled between vendors, because there are no other vendors.

03

Meet members on their rails

Smartphone app, web portal, USSD for feature phones, and the branch teller — the same balance, updated once, visible everywhere.

04

Prove everything, forever

Every birr traceable to a person and a second. When the regulator asks, the answer is a report, not a search party.
